About Disney+ Rip-Off

Disney+ Rip-Off is the dedicated streaming home for EP.NTUN 12 stories. It brings together blockbuster Hollywood movies and award-winning content from SWU, KMITL, NTUN, SWR, Mahidol, and many more, which also includes blockbusters from leading Asian and Thai universities. Available on compatible internet connected devices, Disney+ Rip-Off offers commercial-free viewing with a variety of original feature-length films, live-action and animated series, short-form content and documentaries, including series like Toi, ToiJikVison, Bangkok Revenger and films like Fiat: NTR Story, Yoi Mhoi Ja and Maleficent: Mistress of Evil.
